About Us

Our Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 2015, Spoonful has been serving discerning guests with exceptional fine dining experiences. Our journey began with a simple yet profound vision: to create a sanctuary where culinary excellence meets unparalleled hospitality.

With over a decade of prestigious culinary experience, our founder Chef Michel established Spoonful as the premier destination for connoisseurs seeking authentic, innovative cuisine crafted with only the finest, most exclusive ingredients from around the world.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional culinary experiences that delight our guests and create lasting memories through innovative dishes and impeccable service.

Our Vision

To be the most respected fine dining restaurant in the region, known for our commitment to quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction.

Our Values

Excellence, Innovation, Integrity, and Hospitality guide every decision we make and every dish we serve.
